Beef Drop Flank Stew White Radish (牛腩焖萝卜)
Ingredients Amounts
Beef drop flank (牛腩) 1 pound
White radish (白萝卜) 1
Red Pepper (红辣椒) 3 pieces
Ginger (姜) couple slices
Star anise (八角) 4
Lao Gan Ma (老干妈) 1 teaspoon
Chu hou sauce (柱侯酱) 2 teaspoon
Peanut oil (花生油) 2 teaspoon
Soy Source (生抽) 3 teaspoon
Oyster sauce (蚝油) 3 teaspoon
Green Onion (葱) 1

    Steps

  1. Prepare a small pot of boiling water
  2. Put the uncut beef flank into boiling water for 3 minutes
  3. After 3 minues, put the pre-cooked beef flank into cold water
  4. After it's cool down, cut the beef flank into pieces, any size you like
  5. Add Peatnut oil and place all the ingredients into a big pot
  6. Fry all the ingredients for 1 minute
  7. Add 3 bowls of water to just above the ingredients
  8. After 15 minutes reduce heaat to moderate-low and let it stew for 2 hours
